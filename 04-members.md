# Members

## Overview

The **Members** section shows customer profiles for people who have signed up on the public site. Not all customers have a member profile — some just check out as guests.

## Finding a Member

1. Go to **Members** → **Members List**
2. Search by:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Member ID

## Member Profile

Click a member's name to see:

- **Contact info** — email, phone, address
- **Registration date** — when they signed up
- **Order history** — all orders they've placed
- **Profile details** — age range, health goals, etc. (they filled this out)
- **Documents** — any ID docs or proof of purchase uploaded
- **Calls & Contact** — call history, chat transcripts, email threads
- **Subscription status** — are they an active member? On pause?

## Editing Member Details

If a member asks you to update their info:

1. Click the **Edit** button on their profile
2. Update fields (email, phone, address, etc.)
3. Click **Save**

Changes are logged in the audit trail.

> **Note:** Members can also update most of their info on the public site themselves. The admin portal is for staff corrections.

## Member Notes

Add notes to a member's profile to track:
- Why they paused their subscription
- Known preferences or allergies
- Complaints or issues
- Follow-up reminders

Click **Add Note** and type your note. Other staff will see it next time they view the profile.

## Call & Chat History

Under **Calls & Contact**, you can see:

- All calls made to/from this member (if enabled)
- Chat transcripts from the website
- Email records if integrated
- Dates and duration of calls

Use this to understand customer history before responding to their questions.

## Viewing Documents

Members can upload:
- **ID documents** — proof of identity
- **Order proof** — receipts, payment confirmations

View uploaded documents on their profile under **Documents**. You can:
- Download files
- Approve or reject (if verification is needed)
- Add notes ("ID expired," "Uploaded successfully")

## Subscription Management

If a member manages their own subscription on the public site, you'll see:

- **Status** — Active, Paused, Cancelled
- **Next renewal date**
- **Pause reasons** — why they paused (if provided)

> **Note:** Most subscription changes happen on the public site. Only edit in the admin portal if the system is broken.
