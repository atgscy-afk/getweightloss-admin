# Getting Started

## Logging In

1. Open the admin portal in your browser
2. Click **Sign in with your work account**
3. You'll be redirected to Microsoft/Entra ID — enter your work email and password
4. You'll be redirected back to the portal dashboard

> **Tip:** If you see "Access Denied," contact your admin. Your account needs to be set up with the right permissions.

## The Dashboard

When you log in, you see the **main menu** on the left:

- **Sales** → Leads, Agents, Commissions
- **Orders** → List, search, view details, refunds
- **Members** → Customer profiles and contact history
- **Appointments** → Schedule and view member appointments
- **Chats** → Review member conversations
- **Documents** → Manage ID docs and proof of purchase

## What Can You Do?

Your access depends on your **role and permissions**:

| Role | Can do | Cannot do |
|------|--------|----------|
| **Sales Agent** | View leads, assign to self, see own commissions | Issue refunds, edit member details |
| **Support Agent** | View orders, update status, view members, chat history | Assign leads, process refunds |
| **Support Manager** | Everything support agents can do + issue refunds | Assign leads |
| **Admin** | Everything | (nothing — admin can do it all) |

**Not sure what you can do?** Try clicking a menu. If a section is grayed out or you can't click a button, it means your permissions don't allow it.

## Key Concepts

### Customers vs. Members

- **Customer** = someone who placed an order (in the Perch database)
- **Member** = a registered user on the public site with a profile
  - A customer *becomes* a member when they sign up

### Leads

A **lead** is a customer with **no orders yet**. Sales agents find leads and assign them to close deals.

### Order Status

Orders flow through statuses: **Pending** → **Processing** → **Shipped** → **Complete**

You can update an order's status and add notes. Some statuses trigger automatic actions (like sending notifications to customers).

### What's a "Hold"?

A **hold** (or delay flag) is a note on an order to pause fulfillment. Example: waiting for customer payment confirmation, or waiting for a document.
