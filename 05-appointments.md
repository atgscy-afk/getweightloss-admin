# Appointments

## Overview

**Appointments** lets support staff schedule calls, check-ins, or consultations with members. This is useful for one-on-one guidance, progress reviews, or troubleshooting.

## Viewing Appointments

1. Go to **Appointments** → **Appointments List**
2. See all scheduled appointments in calendar or list view
3. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - Member name
   - Staff member
   - Status (scheduled, completed, cancelled)

## Scheduling an Appointment

1. Click **New Appointment**
2. Fill in:
   - **Member** — who is it for?
   - **Date & time** — when?
   - **Duration** — 15 min, 30 min, 1 hour?
   - **Type** — call, consultation, progress check, other
   - **Notes** — what's it about? (optional)
   - **Staff member** — who's conducting it? (your name or colleague)
3. Click **Save**

A calendar invite is sent to the member and assigned staff.

## Completing an Appointment

After the appointment:

1. Find it in the list
2. Click **Mark as Completed**
3. Add **completion notes**:
   - What you discussed
   - Action items (member to do, staff to follow up)
   - Next steps
4. Click **Save**

These notes are logged on the member's profile for future reference.

## Cancelling an Appointment

If plans change:

1. Click the appointment
2. Click **Cancel**
3. Add a **cancellation reason**
4. The member is notified

## Appointment Types

| Type | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Call** | Phone consultation with member |
| **Progress Check** | Review their health progress |
| **Consultation** | Expert advice or plan review |
| **Troubleshooting** | Help with an issue (order, product, etc.) |
