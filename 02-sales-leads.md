# Sales & Leads

## Overview

The Sales section helps you find new customers (leads) and track your progress. **A lead is a customer who hasn't placed an order yet.**

## Finding Leads

1. Go to **Sales** → **Leads**
2. You'll see a list of unassigned customers with filters:

### Filters

| Filter | Does | Example |
|--------|------|---------|
| **Search** | Find by name, email, or phone | Type "john" to find John Smith |
| **Date Range** | Show only recent sign-ups | Select "Last 7 days" for this week's new customers |
| **Status** | Show leads by assignment status | "Unassigned" = no agent yet, "All" = everything |

**Example workflow:**
- Set **Date Range** to "Last 7 days"
- Set **Status** to "Unassigned"
- Click **Search** to see this week's new unassigned leads

## Assigning a Lead

When you find a lead you want to work:

1. Click the **Assign** button on that customer's row
2. Select which agent to assign it to:
   - **Your own name** = you'll work this lead
   - **Another agent's name** = you're passing it to them
3. Click **Confirm**

✅ **Success:** The lead moves from "Unassigned" to "Assigned to [Agent Name]"

### Why Assign?

Assigning a lead:
- **Tracks ownership** — everyone knows who owns each lead
- **Prevents duplicates** — other agents won't work the same lead
- **Tracks commissions** — the agent gets credit if it converts to an order

## Understanding Status

After you assign a lead, it tracks progress:

- 🔴 **Unassigned** → No agent is working it yet
- 🟡 **Assigned** → An agent is working this customer
- 🟢 **Converted** → They placed an order! (They move to the Orders section)

## View an Assigned Lead

Click the customer's **name** to see:
- Contact info (email, phone)
- Registration date
- Any notes from other agents
- When they were assigned
- To whom they were assigned

## Commissions

Go to **Sales** → **Commissions** to see:

- How many leads you assigned this month
- How many converted to orders
- Your commission for each converted sale
- Total pending commission

> **Tip:** Commission is calculated when a lead converts to an order. This can take a few days to process.

## What Happens After You Assign?

You might:
1. **Contact the customer** — Call, email, or chat to discuss products
2. **Send a quote** — Give them pricing and options
3. **Follow up** — Check back if they don't respond
4. **Close the deal** — Customer places an order (converts)

If a customer places an order, they move from **Leads** to **Orders** automatically.

## Common Questions

**Q: What if a customer doesn't respond?**  
A: You can leave a note on their profile and reassign to another agent if needed.

**Q: Can I reassign a lead?**  
A: Yes — go to the lead and click **Reassign** to move it to another agent.

**Q: Why can't I assign this lead?**  
A: You might not have the `sales.assign` permission. Contact your manager.
