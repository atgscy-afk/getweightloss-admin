# Common Tasks

This page covers skills that work across multiple sections.

## Searching

Most pages have a **Search** box at the top. You can search by:

- **Name** — customer/member first or last name
- **Email** — full email address or partial
- **Phone** — full or partial phone number
- **Order/Reference #** — order number from receipt
- **ID** — member ID or order ID number

### Search Tips

- **Partial matches work** — type "john" to find "john," "johnny," "johnson"
- **Try different fields** — if name doesn't work, try email
- **No special characters** — just type plain text

## Filtering

After searching, use **Filters** to narrow results:

**Common filters:**
- **Date Range** — "Last 7 days," "Last 30 days," custom dates
- **Status** — filter by state: pending, complete, shipped, etc.
- **Assigned to** — show results for one agent/team member

Click the filter, select options, hit **Apply** or **Search**.

## Sorting & Ordering

Click a **column header** to sort:

- Click once → sort A-Z (or oldest-newest)
- Click again → sort Z-A (or newest-oldest)
- A small arrow shows the sort direction

**Example:** Click "Order Date" to sort orders by date.

## Adding Notes

Throughout the system, you'll see **Notes** fields:

- When closing an order: "Refunded — customer changed mind"
- When approving a document: "ID verified"
- On a member profile: "Prefers email over phone"

**Good notes:**
- ✅ Are specific and clear
- ✅ Explain *why* something happened
- ✅ Help other staff understand context

**Bad notes:**
- ❌ "OK" (too vague)
- ❌ "URGENT!!!" (no info)
- ❌ "customer issue" (which issue?)

## Bulk Actions

On list pages, you can select multiple items:

1. Click the **checkbox** next to items you want to act on
2. A **Bulk Actions** menu appears at the top
3. Common actions:
   - **Assign** — give multiple leads to an agent
   - **Export** — download as CSV
   - **Tag** — add a label for organization

## Exporting Data

Need to pull data into a spreadsheet?

1. Select items (or leave blank to export all)
2. Click **Export** → **CSV**
3. File downloads to your computer
4. Open in Excel or Google Sheets

> **Tip:** CSV exports include all visible columns. Use filters first to export only the data you need.

## Audit Trail

Every change in the system is logged. To see what happened:

1. Go to the record (order, member, etc.)
2. Scroll to **Audit Trail** or **Change Log** (bottom of page)
3. You'll see:
   - Who made the change
   - What changed
   - When
   - Why (if they left a note)

Audit logs cannot be edited or deleted.

## Permissions & Access

If you see a **button is grayed out** or a section is **locked:**

- You likely don't have permission for that action
- **Example:** Refunds are only for managers/admins
- Contact your admin or manager to request access

To check your current permissions:
1. Click your **profile** (top right)
2. Click **My Permissions**
3. See what you can do

## Browser Tips

- **Use bookmark folders** — save your frequent pages for quick access
- **Open links in new tab** — right-click → "Open in new tab" to keep lists open
- **Keyboard shortcuts:**
  - `Ctrl/Cmd + F` → find text on page
  - `Ctrl/Cmd + Click` → open link in background tab
  - `Escape` → close modal/dialog

## Performance

If the system feels slow:

1. Try **clearing browser cache** (`Ctrl+Shift+Delete` or menu → clear cache)
2. Use **filters** to reduce data loaded (don't search all 10,000 orders at once)
3. Try a different browser if one is sluggish
4. Contact IT if it's consistently slow
